BERVADO — Brand & Website Guideline

1. Brand Overview

BERVADO is an “Affordable Old Money” clothing brand focused on quiet elegance, minimalism, and timeless style. The brand avoids flashy elements and emphasizes subtle luxury through design, color, and presentation.

---

2. Brand Positioning

- Category: Smart Casual / Old Money Aesthetic
- Target: Males 18–35
- Price Tier: Mid-range (Accessible luxury)
- Core Value: Elegance without noise

---

3. Brand Personality

- Calm
- Confident
- Refined
- Minimal
- Non-flashy

---

4. Logo Usage

Primary Logo:

BERVADO (All Caps Serif)

Style Rules:

- Use serif font
- Wide letter spacing
- Always centered or left-aligned
- Avoid effects (no shadows, no gradients)

Monogram:

BV (used on products, icons, tags)

---

5. Color Palette

Primary Colors:

- Cream: #F5F1E8
- Navy: #0F1A2A

Secondary Colors:

- Olive: #6B705C
- Brown: #4B3A2F

Accent:

- Gold: #C2A878

Rules:

- Backgrounds: Cream or White
- Text: Navy or Black
- Accent: Use Gold sparingly

---

6. Typography

Headings:

Serif (Elegant)
Example:

- Playfair Display
- Cormorant Garamond

Body Text:

Sans Serif (Clean)
Example:

- Open Sans
- Lato

Rules:

- No more than 2 font families
- Headings large and spaced
- Body text readable (16px–18px)

---

7. Visual Style

Photography:

- Natural lighting
- Soft shadows
- Neutral tones
- Locations:
  - Villas
  - Classic interiors
  - Outdoor calm scenes

Models:

- Clean look
- Minimal expressions
- No exaggerated poses

---

8. Brand Voice

Tone:

- Short
- Calm
- Confident

Examples:

- “Timeless Elegance.”
- “Refined Simplicity.”
- “Bervado — Style that speaks quietly.”

---

9. Website Structure

Pages:

- Home
- Shop
- Product Details
- About
- Contact

---

10. Homepage Layout

Sections:

1. Hero Section

- Full screen image
- Text:
  “Timeless Elegance”
- Button: Shop Now

2. Featured Products

- 4–8 items grid

3. Collection Highlight

- Banner image + short text

4. Brand Statement

- Short paragraph about elegance

5. Footer

---

11. Product Page

Must Include:

- Product images (clean background)
- Title
- Price
- Size selector
- Add to cart
- Short description

Description Style:

Short, minimal, no exaggeration

Example:
“Crafted for everyday refinement. Lightweight, breathable, and timeless.”

---

12. UI / UX Rules

- Plenty of white space
- No clutter
- Smooth transitions
- Hover effects subtle
- Buttons simple (no heavy styling)

---

13. Buttons

Primary:

- Background: Navy
- Text: White

Secondary:

- Border: Navy
- Text: Navy

---

14. Cards (Products)

- White background
- Soft shadow
- Image centered
- Minimal text

---

15. Navigation Bar

- Transparent or white
- Logo left
- Links right:
  - Shop
  - About
  - Contact

Sticky on scroll

---

16. Footer

- Minimal links
- Social icons
- Copyright:
  © Bervado 2026

---

17. Technical Stack ()


website :

- ASP.NET Core mvc

Database:

- sqlite

---

18. Performance Rules

- Images optimized
- Lazy loading
- Fast loading (<2s)

---

19. Do NOT Do

- No flashy animations
- No bright colors
- No crowded layout
- No large logos on products

---

20. Final Principle

BERVADO is not about showing wealth.
It is about suggesting it.

If it feels loud — it’s wrong.
If it feels simple but confident — it’s right.
